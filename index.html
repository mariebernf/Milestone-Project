Classes

Attending our excercise classes is great way to stay motivated on your journey and target specific fitness goals.

Some of new classes for 2025, include Yoga and Pilates.

Yoga is a mind-body practice that involves breathing excercises, strength poses and stretches. There are many benifits of Yoga including improved flexibility, reduced stress, improves core strength and promotes mental relaxtion and focus.

Pilates is a low impact excercise focusing on core strength and flexibility. The benifits of pilates include strengthening of the abdominal and back muscles. Improved posture, flexibility and mobility. Strengthens muscles and improves joint function.
